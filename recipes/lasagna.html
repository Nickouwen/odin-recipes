Lasagna

Description

This is the famous dish from the Garfield cartoon. It is called lasagna.
Lasagna is a baked dish comprised of a meat sauce similar to a bolognese, a cheese or white sauce, and flat lasagna noodles. These components are layered in a dish and baked in the oven, creating the iconic layered look of lasagna.

Ingredients

(Ingredients List Taken from keviniscooking):

    Lasagna:
  • 12oz lasagna noodles
  • 1lb mozarella
  • 8oz parmesan cheese
    • Marinara Sauce:
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup dry red wine (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 28oz crushed San Marzano tomatoes
  • 12 fresh basil leaves (finely chopped)
    • Bechamel Sauce:
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/8 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/8 tsp white pepper
    • Meat Sauce:
  • 1lb lean ground beef
  • 1lb hot italian sausage (casing removed)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp fennel seeds
  • 2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 6 cloves minced garlic
  • 1 large white onion
  • Marinara sauce (made in above recipe)

Steps

    Marinara Sauce:
  1. In a large skillet, sautee garlic for 2 mins over med-high heat
  2. Reduce heat to med. Add the wine, tomato paste, sugar, oregano, salt and red pepper flakes. Sautee for 2 mins
  3. Add the tomatoes, stir and let simmer for 20 minutes. In the final 10 mins, add basil and stir
  4. If you want a smooth sauce, pulse in a blender or using a hand blender, blend until desired consistency
    Bechamel Sauce:
  1. Warm the milk in a saucepan
  2. In a medium saucepan melt equal parts butter and then the flour with the salt and white pepper
  3. Whisk the flour and butter together, allowing to simmer on low for 1-2 minutes to slightly cook
  4. Gradually add the warm milk and whisk until a smooth sauce forms on low heat. Cook until sauce has thickened and coats back of spoon
    Meat Sauce:
  1. In a large skillet over med-high heat, add olive oil, then red pepper flakes and fennel seeds. Sautee for 30 seconds then add the chopped onion. Continue on med-high heat until onion is soft (2 mins), then add the garlic, stirring for 30 seconds
  2. Add the ground beef and sausage. Brown meat over med-high heat, breaking meat apart with spatula
  3. Add marinara sauce to meat mixture, stirring to combine
  4. Cover and allow to simmer for 20 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally
    Lasagna Noodles:
  1. In a 9x13 baking dish, add the dry lasagna noodles and cover with hot water. Allow to rest 20 minutes. Drain into collander
    Assembly:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease 9x13 baking dish with cooking spray
  2. Start by spreading meat sauce on the bottom of the dish. Put a layer of lasagna noodles on top to cover the meat sauce
  3. Spread more meat sauce over the noodles, followed by the bechamel sauce, then some mozzarella and parmesan cheese
  4. Repeat previous step 3-4 times until noodles are used up. End with a layer of cheese and white sauce on top
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling. Allow to rest for 15 minutes after removing from oven
